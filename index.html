About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. I am a passionate and versatile artist with a diverse set of skills spanning graphic design, charcoal art, painting, and layout design. My creative journey is fueled by a love for bringing visual concepts to life, whether through digital media or traditional art forms.

Beyond my artistic expertise, I also have a deep appreciation for movement and expression through dance, constantly seeking new ways to fuse creativity and passion in all that I do. My work is a reflection of my commitment to excellence and my ability to adapt and innovate across various artistic disciplines.

I believe in the power of art to inspire, provoke, and transform, and I am always looking for new opportunities to collaborate and push the boundaries of my craft. Whether it's creating stunning visuals, designing compelling layouts, or expressing emotion through dance, I strive to bring my full creative potential to every project.

Projects

Malaysian Concept

A custom Yamaha Mio i 125 modified with a sleek Malaysian-inspired design. Featuring blue, white, and black accents, this build includes performance upgrades such as Mutakin Enkie wheels, a 330mm rear shock, and a Brembo brake system.

Charcoal Art

Meticulously crafted charcoal artworks that explore shading, texture, and contrast. Each piece tells a powerful emotional story through monochrome depth and delicate contrasts of light and dark.

ID Lace

A refined collection of minimalist lanyard designs tailored for branding and promotional use. Clean layouts combined with modern typography ensure a professional and stylish presentation for corporate or personal events.

Doodle Paintings
